How It Works

Sign Up

Quick registration with phone OTP. Verify your identity to start sharing.

2

Add Books

Snap a photo, add title/author. Choose to donate or exchange.

3

Explore books near you. Chat with owners to arrange pickup.

4

Meet & Exchange

Meet at a safe public place. Complete the exchange and rate.

Safety First

  • Phone Verification: All users verified via OTP
  • Community Ratings: Rate and review other users
  • Safe Meetup: In-app location sharing for handoffs
  • Report & Block: Report inappropriate behavior instantly
  • Verified Badge: Earn trust through successful exchanges
  • Emergency Support: Report safety concerns to our team

Our Commitment

Boichokro is committed to creating a safe and inclusive community for all book lovers. We take safety seriously and enforce strict community guidelines.

Every user is verified, exchanges are transparent, and our moderation team works 24/7 to ensure a positive experience for everyone.
