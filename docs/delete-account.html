Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your right to privacy and data ownership. You can easily delete your account and all associated personal data directly within the Boichokro mobile app.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your messages, book listings, and profile data will be permanently erased.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Boichokro app and ensure you are logged in.
  2. Tap on the Profile icon in the bottom navigation menu.
  3. Open Settings from your Profile page.
  4. Scroll down and tap the red Delete Account button.
  5. Confirm your decision in the popup dialog.

What happens when you delete your account?

Upon confirming deletion, our systems will immediately and permanently erase:

  • Your Profile Data (Name, Phone Number, Profile Photo)
  • All your Book Listings and associated cover images
  • Your chat history and records
